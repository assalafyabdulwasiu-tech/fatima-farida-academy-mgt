Admission Process

1. Application

Pick up an admission form at our Kwabenya campus office for GHS 0.00.

2. Assessment

Students will undergo a basic placement interview or test to determine their level.

3. Enrollment

Submit required documents (Birth Certificate, Vaccination records) and pay the commitment fee.

Required Documents

  • ✅ Original and Photocopy of Birth Certificate
  • ✅ Previous School Report (for Primary/JHS)
  • ✅ Two (2) Passport-sized Photographs
